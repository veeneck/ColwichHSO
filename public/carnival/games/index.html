★ CARNIVAL GAMES ★

This year we have 13 games for you to enjoy! Completing these games will win you prizes in the Prize Room. You can also find more fun and prizes in our additional Activities!

  • 1.

    1. Corn Hole

    Win a ticket when you sink a bean bag.

  • 2.

    2. Nerf Targets

    Shoot Nerf targets and win tickets.

  • 3.

    3. Plinko

    Win tickets based on where your Plinko lands.

  • 4.

    4. Climbing Wall

    Touch the top of the wall and get a ticket!

  • 5.

    5. Axe Throw

    Throw an axe at the target, and win the tickets.

  • 6.

    6. Basketball Shot

    The further the shot, the more tickets you win.

  • 7.

    7. Ultimate Shot

    Can you toss one ball into each tube? Tickets to be won.

  • 8.

    8. Duck Pond

    Catch three ducks and add ticket numbers together.

  • 9.

    9. Gone Fishing

    Have fun fishing, but replace the fish with candy.

  • 10.

    10. Chicken Toss

    Throw a chicken, hit a target, win some tickets!

  • 11.

    11. Pop Toss

    Throw a ring around a can of soda and win tickets!

  • 12.

    12. Baseball Toss

    Throw the baseball through the hole and win a ticket!

  • 13.

    13. Sucker Pull

    Pull a lollipop out of the wall, and color coordinate it to tickets.

★ ACTIVITIES ★

We’ll also have these additional activities to enjoy! While they don’t give out Prize Room tickets, they do have their own prizes to be won.

  • 1.

    1. Dessert Walk 🧁

    Grab a spot on the dessert walk! If the wheel stops on your number, you win!

  • 2.

    2. Bounce House 🦘

    Bounce the night away. And new this year is the obstacle bounce course!

  • 3.

    3. Face Painting & Tattoo 🎨

    Add a little Carnival spirit to your face with colorful paintings.

  • 4.

    4. Twisted Balloons 🎈

    Get into the Carnival Spirit with Balloon Animals!

  • 5.

    5. Bingo 🅱️

    The game that we all know and love. Prizes to be won each round!

  • 6.

    6. Candy Count 🍬

    Who can guess how many pieces of candy are in the jar? Closest wins!

  • 7.

    7. Velcro Wall 🤪

    Strap on the velcro suit, take a bounce, and see how high you stick!

  • 8.

    8. Photo Booth 📷

    Remember the night with photos of your family and friends!

★ Prize Room ★

Win tickets by playing the Carnival Games, and redeem them in the prize room. When you go through admissions, you’ll be given a punch card necklace. Upon completing each game, your necklace will get a punch. Prize room closes at 7:30pm.
