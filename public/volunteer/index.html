Ways To Help

A few of the ways to get involved.

  • Work An Event

    See where help is needed at the next meeting, or fill out this membership form to offer your time for an event.

  • Baked Goods

    Teacher Appreciation, Dessert Social and Coffee With Cardinals are just a few of the events that could use baked goods.

  • Room Parent

    Consider helping your teachers and HSO by becoming a Room Parent for your child's classroom.

  • Attend a Meeting

    Check out our upcoming meetings to see if you can attend any. Your input is appreciated!
