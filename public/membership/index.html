Fill out the form below to register as an active HSO member, which includes you on communications related to voting and volunteering.

How do I become a HSO member?

Membership is automatically granted to all parents and guardians of Colwich Elementary School students, plus all staff at Colwich Elementary.

What is an active member?

Active members are those who have filled out the form above, which includes them in the different communications lists we have. Active members will be contacted by email and SMS for different purposes like voting on HSO fund usage, volunteering oppurtunities, and so on.

How often will I be contacted?

You should expect to hear from us about once a month. Most communications will be around meeting updates and requests for votes. We’ll also let you know about upcoming events, and ways you can help out.
