What is Spirit Night?

It is when we partner with a restaurant/car wash/skating rink on a particular day and that place donates back a set percentage of sales to Colwich HSO by just purchasing something there and mentioning our school. We have 4 this year!

When are Spirit Nights?

There will be two spirit nights in the fall and two in the spring. The first one is usually in September. The other 3 spirit nights will be determined at a later date. Please be on the lookout for that information.

How much is donated and where does the money go?

The donated money goes to the HSO. The amount donated depends on the partnering business. Some give 10% of the sales while others give more or less.

Who can attend?

Anyone! Please pass the information to your friends, family and relatives. More the merrier!

What do you have to purchase?

This depends on the partnering business. Details will be given for each spirit night. Check the website or be on the lookout for upcoming announcements.
