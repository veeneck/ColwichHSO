Teacher Request For Funds

We've Got You Covered üè¶

Teachers, HSO would love to be able to fund your request for field trips, classroom projects, or other financial needs. Please read the Fund Request FAQ, and then submit the form below!

How are requests under $500 handled?

Once you submit the form above, your request will go through a vote with the current HSO officers. You should hear back on the status of your request within a week of submitting it.

What if the request is over $500?

Requests over $500 will require a vote from all HSO members. We will look more at our yearly budget and goals when considering this request.

I submitted a request, but haven't heard back from anyone.

If more than a week has passed since you submitted your request, please contact a HSO Officer. You can also fill out this Contact Form.
