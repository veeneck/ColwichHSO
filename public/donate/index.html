How to Donate

The different ways you can donate.

  • Donate Your Time

    If you're interested in volunteering, please consider becoming a member. We would love to have you!

  • Teacher Wishlist

    You can purchase school supplies off of the teacher's Amazon wishlists to directly support them.

  • No Overhead

    Colwich HSO is completely run by volunteers, so all fundraising and donations benefit the students.

  • Monetary Donation

    We greatly appreciate donations of any amount. 100% of the money will go directly to supporting the school.

Are there any quick ways I can help out?

We understand that not everyone has time in their schedule to come to meetings, or be there at daytime hours. If you’re just looking to help out, please consider one of the following options:

  • Purchase a gift from the teachers Amazon wish lists.
  • Volunteer for an event.
  • Provide baked goods for an event.
  • Donate to our cause!
  • Set us as your Amazon Smile charity.
  • Set us as your community rewards when you shop at Dillions.

How are donations used?

Donations go back to the school in a variety of forms. Field trips, supplies and teacher appreciation are examples of recurring expenses. We also save towards larger expenses. In recent years, we have purchased a new sound system for the school, and the digital marquee out front. We greatly appreciate any donation you have to offer.

Does HSO have any overhead?

Colwich HSO is 100% run by volunteers, so you can be assured that your entire donation will go back towards the school, teachers and children.

Is HSO a non profit?

Yes! HSO was established in 2013 as a 501(c)3 non-profit. The EIN number is 46-4417245.
