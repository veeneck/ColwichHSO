Prepare for the new school year with our used uniform sales. Stop by on July 29th and July 30th, from 8am to Noon, to shop used uniforms.

What is the used uniform sale?

The Used Uniform Sale is a chance for you to sell your old uniforms to incoming families.

When and where is the used uniform sale?

This happens every year on the day of enrollment at the front lobby of CES. For the 2024-25 school year, it‚Äôs on July 31st from 8 am - 6 pm.

Who can buy?

Anyone who has a child attending CES.

Who can sell?

Anyone who has CES school approved uniforms that they no longer need or want.

What does the seller have to do?

If you plan to sell, please bring your clean uniforms to the lobby before the sale on July 31st. Make sure you pin an envelope on each item with the price, size and full name. There will be HSO volunteers to help collect and monitor the envelopes.

How much do uniforms usually cost?

The price depends on what the individual seller wants for their item. They are typically discounted compared to purchasing them new.

When do I get my money if I was a seller?

You can pick up your money on the day of or in the office anytime after the uniform sale day.
