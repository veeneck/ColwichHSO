What is the Teacher Appreciation Cart?

It is a small 3-tiered cart located in the teacher’s lounge that is filled with goodies for the CES staff. HSO refills it 3 times a year.

What is on the cart?

It can be anything from consumable items like fresh fruit, chips, nuts, candy to post-it, markers or even chapstick and lotions.

How can I help?

If you want to donate items, please contact us or let one of the officers know. Teachers are grateful for anything!
