There are prizes for 1st, 2nd and 3rd place classes.

1st place:

  • The winning class will enjoy a Pizza Ranch trip, dress down day, extra recess, and silly string their teacher!

2nd place:

  • Second place gets a pizza party, non uniform item and extra recess!
  • Second place teacher will have thier lunch delivered along with a $75 amazon card for the classroom.

3rd place:

  • Third place wins an ice cream party and non uniform item!
  • Third place teacher receives a a $50 amazon card for the classroom.
