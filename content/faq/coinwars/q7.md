type: faq
faq_category: coinwars

There are prizes for 1st, 2nd and 3rd place classes.

1st place:

- The winning class will enjoy a Pizza Ranch trip and a dress down day! 
- The winning teacher receives a $100 Amazon card for the classroom.

2nd place:

- Second place wins a pizza party and a dress down day! 
- Second place teacher receives a $75 Amazon card for the classroom.

3rd place:

- Third place gets an ice cream party and a dress down day! 
- Third place teacher receives a $50 Amazon card for the classroom.
