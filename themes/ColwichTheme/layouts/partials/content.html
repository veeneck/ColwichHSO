★ How are the proceeds used? ★

All funds raised from this event will directly benefit your kids and the school. HSO pays for field trips, classroom improvements, teacher appreciation, and much more. Completely run by volunteers, HSO has no overhead. 100% of profits will be used to improve Colwich Elementary School.

★ Where do I park? ★

Carnival will take place in Colwich Elementary School located at 401. S Marion St. Entry will be on the West side of the building where the pre-school classrooms are located. Parking will be available in that area. For more information, have a look at our Carnival Map.

■ Which payment types are accepted? ■

Cash & Credit Cards will be accepted at the entrance, concessions, and raffle / auction table. Entry is $10 per child. The maximum amount that one family will pay for entry is $30. Adults and children under 2 get free entry.
