All funds raised from this event will directly benefit your kids and the school. HSO pays for field trips, classroom improvements, teacher appreciation, and much more. ★ Where do I park? ★

Carnival will take place in Colwich Elementary School located at 401. S Marion St. Entry will be on the West side of the building where the pre-school classrooms are located. Parking will be available in that area. For more information, check out our Carnival Map!

 

■ Which payment types are accepted? ■

Cash, Credit Cards & Checks will be accepted at the entrance, concessions, and raffle / auction table. Entry is $10 per child. The maximum amount that one family will pay for entry is $30. Adults and children under 2 get free entry.
